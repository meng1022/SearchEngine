<P>
Fossil fuels include coal, petroleum, natural gas, oil shales, bitumens, tar sands, and heavy oils. 
All contain carbon and were formed as a result of geologic processes acting 
on the remains of organic matter produced by photosynthesis, a process that began in the Archean Eon (4.0 billion to 2.5 billion years ago).
